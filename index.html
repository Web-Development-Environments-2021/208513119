About Me

Hey everyone and welcome to my site, actually my first one !

My name is Nelly Edlin I am 83 years old and still counting, I was born in Poltava, Ukraine on the 11th of September, 1937.I was born to Elena Vinarsky and Haim Gaysinsky. I have 2 sisters, Maya is 85 years old today, she lives nowdays with her family in Berlin, Germany. Nina was my sister but from another father and she died because of a cancer when she was only 18 years old, it was shortly after the war end.

I came from a middle-class family, my mom was a Polish woman and a housewife, my dad was a CEO of a Commercial Network in Ukraine. When World War || began, my father as a Ukrainian citizen belonging to the Soviet Union at the time,volunteered to go to the war from which he never returned. I was only 4 years old when the war came into Ukraine and I remember everything like it's was yesterday ! We were sent to labor camps in Uzbekistan with my mother and my 2 sisters, and survived there all together until the war ended and the Red Army won the Nazis. We came back to Poltava after the war ended and we soon became a poor jewish family, moved into basements with gypsies. When I was 17 I graduated school and went to the university in Poltave there I recieved my Master Degree in Architecture.

While studing in the university I met my husband Gregory. We got married on the 9th of April, 1960. We made an Aliyah to Israel in 1995, and we are married already 61 years ! We have 2 amazing daughters, Irena and Larisa, 4 lovely grandchildren and even 2 sweetest great-grandchildren. My family is the only thing that keeps me alive, I am thankful to God for having what I have in life.
